fix: Zen/Firefox crash by withholding wl_pointer::axis_relative_direction

Bumping `wl_seat` to version 10 natively bumped the advertised `wl_pointer`
version to 10. Zen browser (and Firefox/Gecko) correctly negotiates the
maximum version (10) but currently provides a legacy `wl_pointer_listener`
struct compiled against older headers (missing the 10th callback).

Consequently, when the compositor dispatched the `axis_relative_direction`
event (opcode 10) during scroll, Zen crashed in `libwayland-client` due to
a NULL function pointer dereference.

To work around this client bug and prevent crashes, we now unconditionally
withhold the `axis_relative_direction` event during scrolling routines in
`src/input/mod.rs`.
